What is AgriSynth?

AgriSynth is more than an app; it's a comprehensive Urban Farming Operating System designed to tackle food insecurity and transform urban landscapes into productive, sustainable ecosystems.

The Urban Challenge

Megacities are growing, yet sustainable food access remains a critical issue. AgriSynth addresses:

  • Underutilized urban spaces (rooftops, balconies).
  • Fragmented farming efforts lacking coordination.
  • Absence of reliable tech for soil health and yield tracking.
  • Long, carbon-intensive food supply chains.
  • Vulnerability of centralized food systems.

Our Innovative Solution

AgriSynth provides a modular, scalable, and open platform powered by AI, IoT, blockchain, and automation, governed by communities. It's a digital declaration of regenerative independence.

We believe this inefficiency is not natural. It is systemic. It is solvable.

Core Modules & Features

AgriSynth's power lies in its integrated modules, creating a seamless experience for urban farmers and communities.

AgriSynth Core & GrowAI

  • Real-time data from IoT sensors (soil, weather, crop).
  • AI-driven insights for optimal crop cycles and disease detection.

DroneSync & Automation

  • APIs for drone-based mapping, spraying, surveillance.
  • Tools for automating routine farming tasks.

FarmChain & GreenMarket

  • Blockchain for produce traceability and smart contracts.
  • Integrated marketplace with smart logistics and AI pricing.

GrowNet Community

  • Peer-to-peer network for farmers, buyers, and experts.
  • Platform for exchanging goods, ideas, and skills.

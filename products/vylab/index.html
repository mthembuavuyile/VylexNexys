What is VyLab?

The official VyLab platform features robust educational tools built to elevate high school physical sciences.

Covers essential chemistry and physics experiments aligned with the South African CAPS curriculum (Grades 10-12).

Classroom Analytics

Enables educators to assign specific experiments, monitor learner performance metrics, and export grades from a central dashboard.

No Equipment Needed

Remove the constraints of expensive laboratory equipment. All simulations run smoothly on smartphones, tablets, or school desktops.

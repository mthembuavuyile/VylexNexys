How to Play

Basic Rules

  1. Form at least 2 teams
  2. One player describes words to teammates
  3. 30 seconds to guess as many words as possible
  4. Each correct guess = 1 point
  5. First team to reach target score wins!

Not Allowed

  • Saying any part of the word
  • Using "rhymes with" or "sounds like"
  • Using abbreviations of the word
  • Using gestures/charades
  • Spelling out the word

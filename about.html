About Vylex Nexys

Practical web utilities built for South African learners

What Is Vylex Nexys?

Vylex Nexys is a web platform built under Vylex Pty Ltd that provides free academic productivity tools, APS score calculators, citation builders, and STEM utilities for South African high school and university students.

Navigating Matric exams, university entry requirements, paper citations, and study schedules can often feel overwhelming. Vylex Nexys simplifies these tasks by offering clean, fast, and accessible tools that run directly in your web browser—no expensive software, account paywalls, or heavy downloads required.

What We Offer

Core utilities designed for everyday academic tasks

APS & Admission Checker

Calculate your Admission Point Score (APS) from Grade 12 marks and check eligibility across major South African university programs.

SaCite Citation Builder

Format error-free APA, Harvard, MLA, and Chicago reference citations in seconds for school essays and research papers.

STEM & Study Utilities

Access browser-based chemistry and physics simulators, math equation tools, note editors, and automatic study timetable generators.

About Vylex Nexys

Founded by Mr. Avuyile Mthembu in 2024, Vylex Nexys is a South African technology company dedicated to innovation and digital empowerment. Our mission is to create impactful solutions—from business technology to free educational platforms—that foster growth, accessibility, and opportunity.

With a strong belief in technology as a force for good, we develop websites, apps, and programs that empower individuals and businesses. Whether through digital transformation for companies or philanthropic initiatives that provide free learning resources, we are committed to making a lasting difference.
